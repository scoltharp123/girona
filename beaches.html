Discover the Beaches

Top Costa Brava Beaches to Visit by Bike

Aiguablava Beach

Cycle through rolling hills to reach Aiguablava’s crystal-clear waters and fine golden sand. This sheltered cove is perfect for a refreshing dip after a ride, with stunning Mediterranean views that make every pedal worth it.

Cala Pedrosa

Pedal along coastal paths to this secluded pebble beach. Surrounded by pine forests and rugged cliffs, Cala Pedrosa offers tranquility and a chance to soak in the Mediterranean’s raw beauty.

Sa Caleta

Ride to Sa Caleta, a small sandy beach with calm waters near Lloret de Mar. Its medieval castle backdrop and vibrant marine life make it a cyclist’s paradise for relaxation and snorkeling.

Why Cycle to Costa Brava’s Beaches?

Nothing beats the thrill of pedaling through Girona’s scenic trails, with the Mediterranean breeze guiding you to Costa Brava’s pristine shores. As you crest a hill and spot the sparkling turquoise waters, the sense of accomplishment is unmatched. These beaches, from Aiguablava’s serene coves to Cala Pedrosa’s rugged charm, offer the perfect reward for your ride—whether you’re diving into the sea or relaxing on the sand.

Cycling Routes to the Coast

Girona to Aiguablava

A 40km ride through lush forests and coastal paths. Moderate climbs lead to breathtaking views of Aiguablava’s turquoise waters. Perfect for a full-day adventure.

Palafrugell to Cala Pedrosa

A 15km scenic route along quiet roads and trails. This shorter ride ends at the tranquil Cala Pedrosa, ideal for a quick escape to the coast.
